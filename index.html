About Me

Engineer. Architect.
The Go-To Person.

I'm a Senior Staff Software Engineer at Intuit with over 13 years building fintech products that serve millions of small businesses globally. I was the founding engineer of the EPG (Emerging Products Group) team, responsible for building a new Financing-as-a-Service platform from scratch — bootstrapping the team's entire development infrastructure and onboarding every new engineer who joined.

My work spans the full stack: React/TypeScript frontends, Spring Boot/Kafka/Cassandra microservices, and now production AI systems using RAG, Graph RAG, agentic architectures, and custom ML models. I've decomposed multiple monoliths into scalable microservices, led mission-critical 3P integrations, authored technical architecture decisions across organizations, and shipped features used by millions.

I hold 3 patents, won the PayPal Opportunity Hack, earned a 3.9 GPA in my Master's at SJSU, and have been consistently described by managers and peers as the person they turn to for the hardest, highest-stakes engineering challenges.

AI & ML — Production Systems
Built a native ML email classifier (Murthy.ai) from scratch using Intuit AI Workbench, incorporating merchant, estimate, and transactional context. Filed a patent on LLM-based onboarding.
